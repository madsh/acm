# Bylaws
This document contains the bylaws for a local chapter of Association for Computing Machinery, hereafter referred to as ACM. In addition this document contains the information required for the registration of voluntary organisations in the danish and swedish business registers.

<!-- [en] -->
# Name and location
The name of the organisation is Oeresund Association for Computing Machinery Chapter and is using the local names of ACM Øresund and ACM Öresund. Hereafter referred to as The Chapter.

The ACM is located at 100 West Tenth Street in the City of Wilmington, County of New Castle, USA. The name and address of the resident agent is the Corporation Trust Company, 100 West Tenth Street, Wilmington, Delaware, USA.

The Chapters mailing address is Vesterbrogade 999, 1620 København V, Denmark.

The Chapters e-mail address is hello@acmoeresund.org.

<!-- [da] -->
# Navn og hjemsted
Foreningen navn er ACM Øresund og anvender også navnene The Oeresund Chapter of Association for Computing Machinery eller ACM Öresund.

Foreningens hjemsted er København, Danmark.

# Purpose
The Chapter is organised and will be operated exclusively for educational, artistic and scientific purposes.

The Chapter equally serves swedish and danish interrests.

The Chapters purpose is advancing the art, science, engineering, and application of computing, serving both professional and public interests by fostering the open exchange of information and by promoting the highest professional and ethical standards.

The Chapters purpose aligns with ACMs charter for supporting local chapters.

The full ACM bylaws are available at 

- https://www.acm.org/about-acm/acm-bylaws
- https://www.acm.org/chapters/chapter-policies
- https://www.acm.org/chapters/chapter-membership-requirement

# Membership
All professional members of ACM with a residency in Denmark or Sweden are eligible for memberships of The Chapter.

<!-- [da] -->
# Medlemmer
Alle professions-medlemmer? af ACM (eng. professional members) der er bosat i Danmark eller Sverige kan være medlemmer af foreningen.

<!-- [en] -->
# Board of Directors
The Chapters Board of Directors has three officers; a Chair, Vice-Chair, and Treasurer.

Among the three officers at least one person must have residency in Denmark and one person must have residency in Sweden.

The three officers are appointed at the General Assembly and decides how to appoint the three roles among them.

The unanimous consenting Board of Directors has signing authority for The Chapter.

<!-- [da] -->
#Bestyrelse
Foreningens bestyrelse består af tre personer der vælges på generalforsamling. Bestyrelsen skal bestå af mindst en person bosat i Sverige og mindst en person bosat i Danmark.

Bestyrelsen konstituerer sig med formand (eng. Chair), næstformand (eng. Vice Chair) og kasser (eng. Treasurer).

Foreningen tegnes af den samlede bestyrelse.

<!-- [se] -->

<!-- [en] -->
# Election and General Assembly
The Board of Directors is elected from and by The Chapter	members present at the General Assembly.

The Chapters General Assembly is held no later than 13 months after the previous General Assembly.

Notices of General Assemblies along with the agenda shall be distributed to all members no later than 14 days prior to any General Assembly using the members ACM e-mail adresses.

 The Chapter shall hold an Extraordinary General Assembly at any time at the written request of at least 13 members. Notice and agenda must follow that of the regular General Assembly.

<!-- [da] -->
# Afstemninger og generalforsamlinger
Bestyrelsen vælges af og blandt de fremmødte medlemmer på generalforsamling.

Den ordinære generalforsamling afholdes seneste 13 måneder efter sidste, og indkaldes med dagsorden og fjorten dages varsel via email til medlemmernes ACM mail.

Ekstraordinære geneforsamlinger kan indkaldes såfremt mindst 13 medlemmer ønsker det og dagsorden og varsel følger den ordinære generalforsamling.

<!-- [en] -->
# Publications
The Chapters members can publish in the name of The Chapter.

The Board of Directors must approve all publications in the name of The Chapter.

The Board of Directors can formulate criteria that allows The Chapters members to publish in the name af The Chapter without The Board of Directors approval.

<!-- [da] -->
# Publikationer
Foreningens medlemmer kan udgive tekster i foreninges navn.

Foreningens bestyrelsen skal godkende alle udgivelser i foreningens navn.

Foreningens bestyrelsen kan fastsætte regler der lader foreningens medlemmer udgiver tekster i foreningens navn uden bestyrelsens godkendelse.

<!-- [en] -->
# Dues, finance og sponsorater
The Chapters fiscal year begins on July 1st and closes June 30th.

The Chapters does not collect membership dues and aims to have no monetary transactions.

The Chapter can receive sponsored items that are directly related to The Chapters operation such as, but not limited to, equipment, domains or printing of reports.

<!-- [da] -->
# Kontingent, regnskab og sponsorater
Foreningens medlemmer betaler kontigent til ACM og der opkræves intet selvstændigt kontigent til foreningen.

Foreningen kan ikke modtage eller udbetale penge og fører derfor ikke regnskab.

Foreningen kan modtage sponsorater i form af eksempelvis lån af lokaler, udstyr, domæner eller tryk af materiale.

<!-- [en] -->
# Dissolution

The Chapter can be dissolved by a majority vote at an Extraordinary General Assembly held with sole purpose of dissolving The Chapter.

The Chapter is automatically dissolved if it does not hold a General Assembly within 18 months of the last held General Assembly.

If The Chapter does not carry out any activities in addition to the General Assembly it is automatically dissolved 18 months after it last activity.

When The Chapter is dissolved, its assets and liabilities shall be transferred to ACM under supervision by ACM’s Finance Director. Remaining assets is donated to Red Cross.

<!-- [da] -->
# Opløsning
Hvis foreningen ikke indkalder til og afholder ordinære generalforsamlinger, opløses foreningen automatisk 18 måneder efter sidst afholdte generalforsamling.

Hvis foreningen ikke afholder aktiviter i tillæg til generalforsamling opløses foreningen automatisk 18 måneder efter sidst afholdte aktivitet.

<!-- [en] -->
# Authority
In case of conflict, this Constitution and Bylaws, the policies set by Council, the policies set by the Executive Committee, and the policies set by the ACM Chief Operating Officer take precedence, in the order stated, over any provisions of chapter bylaws or internal policies.
